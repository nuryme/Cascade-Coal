Our Story

Coal, Craft,
and the Cascades.

Cascade & Coal was built from a conviction: that fire—managed with care, fueled with intention—transforms a great cut of beef into something altogether different. We opened on Capitol Hill to bring that conviction to Seattle's table.

Every protein is sourced within a day's drive of the city, or through relationships built over years with the world's few truly exceptional Wagyu importers. Nothing arrives frozen. Nothing leaves underwhelming.

The room is quiet by design. The service is present, not performative. Your table is yours for the evening.
